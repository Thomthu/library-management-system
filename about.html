Online Library Management System


Manual process of keeping student records, book records, account details, managing member is very difficult. There are various problems also faced by the student in Auwa state university library such as finding any particular book, information whether book is available or not, for what time this book will be available, searching of books using books using book id and also updating ,deleting and inserting new member, books and noting borrow details and etc. To eliminate this manual system,online Library Management System has been developed.
